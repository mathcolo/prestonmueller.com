Rebounder

Rebounder is my latest Android app. Free on the Google Play Store, it allows your phone to respond to special SMS messages requesting information. For example, it can listen for "#battery" and automatically respond with your phone's battery level, or "#locate" can return your GPS location (great for parents or friends!).
MathTasks

MathTasks was a fancy equation solver for iPhone OS 3.0+. It began as a summer endeavor with two friends in 2009, and it was downloaded more than 100,000 times in its first year. It still lives in Apple's App Store, but we aren't updating it for iOS 8. SMS Tether

SMS Tether was my first significant open source project. It was a relatively simple, secure pair of apps to respond to Android SMS messages using your Mac over the local network. I wrote it because I was tired of typing on my HTC Incredible's keyboard. It's archived over on Google Code.
